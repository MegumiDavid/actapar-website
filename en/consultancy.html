Consultancy services

ACTAPAR's consultancy services are focused in the creation of an internal innovative environment via a high-end system that generates and gathers ideas

Conceptual lectures to leaders

  • Palestras ministradas à liderança da Empresa abordando os tipos mais comuns de sistemas de geração e captação de ideias.Como um sistema de alto desempenho propulsiona a cultura interna de inovação ao favorecer a criação do Meio Inovador Interno e as vantagens do conceito de pequenas ideias na busca da inovação a partir de todas as pessoas da organização, do general ao soldado raso.
  • Individual interviews to measure the awareness of scope, benefits and expectations that a high-end idea management program can bring to the company.

Awareness of the leaders

Individual and collective training programs with leaders about expectations, postures, project importance, concepts, types of ideas, interpersonal motivation, successful returns, and more;

Pilot test implementation

Low-scale test of a system of ideas before extending it to the whole organization. In organizations with less than 50 people the pilot test involves everyone, but a period of experimentation and learning is introduced before the system of ideas is "officially" launched.

Post-implementation consultancy services

  • Training of all of the company's collaborators and leaders on how to use the system;
  • Specific training to the high-level employees about demands and how to effectively supervise performance;;
  • Periodical performance reports and meetings to evaluate results;
  • Training of the project team to efficiently manage the system;
  • Elaboration of idea-gathering application forms;
  • Development of a WEB environment with the IT team to gather external ideas.
