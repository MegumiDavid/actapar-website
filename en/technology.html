Information Technology

We bring a technology plan that will make registering and supervising ideas easier.

Idea Gathering and Supervising Management System

We consider as high-end idea gathering systems those which generate at least 10 ideas by employee in a year. The management of this kind of system presents huge challenges, requiring a competent and efficient IT platform.

ACTAPAR has developed a management system to gather, supervise and register a big number of ideas and their progress with the use of deadline control tools and management reports, which permits supervision on all levels via an easy, amicable and efficient way. The system was built in a WEB environment with responsive designs, adequate to all devices with access to the Internet and including the gathering of ideas, reviews, polls, and data-providing executions so that the process can be fully administrated.

The company will have access to a safe, exclusive and independent database.
