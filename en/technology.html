We bring a technology plan that will make registering and supervising ideas easier.

Idea Gathering and Supervising Management System

In order to achieve a successful innovation project, registering ideas, monitoring processes and strengthening management data is of paramount importance. And we achieve that quintessentially!

According to international parameters, high-performance suggestion projects are those who can achieve 12 ideas per employee in the time frame of a year. This number generates a big demand of management and actions, and requires a robust technological platform, although one with high usability and good user feedback: after all, our main target audience is the employee in all levels of the company.

We developed a platform perfectly aligned with the concepts and innovation culture aimed to be implemented in our clients, with designs and customizations adequate to the reality of the company, as the identification of the project as part of the company's strategy, and not as a "suggestion box", is of extreme importance.

It is not about a simple computer system, but about an intelligent platform that can not only administer processes in all levels, from the caption to the execution and definition of special ideas, but also provides tools for immediate decision-making, which contributes to the progression of the project. Not to mention the responsiveness of the platform, having support in all types of digital devices - be it a phone, a tablet or a computer.

Worthy of note: our platform permits the individual control of the units pertaining to your company (separated and consolidated controls), and supports various languages.

We take care of hosting the platform, doing the appropriate maintenance, and keeping your data secure. Schedule now a demonstration meeting of the best processes and culture to implant in your company. The power of innovation resides in the small ideas! Have you already sent your ideas today?
